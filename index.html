Benefits

At Cengage Learning we are accountable to and for each other. Part of that is recognizing the commitment, talent and skills of our employees by offering a comprehensive benefits plan and program.

Eligibility

As a full-time employee scheduled to work 30 or more hours per week, you become eligible for most benefits on your date of hire.

You may also enroll eligible dependents in many plans. Along with your spouse/domestic partner and children up to age 26, eligible dependents also include:

  • • stepchildren
  • • grandchildren
  • • adopted children
  • • children for whom you or your spouse is legal guardian
  • • children of domestic partners

Cengage Learning employees receive:

  • • Medical, dental and vision insurance
  • • Health savings and flexible spending accounts
  • • Short- and long-term disability insurance
  • • Life and AD&D insurance
  • • Dependent life insurance
  • • Individual and family AD&D
  • • Legal expense insurance
  • • 401(k) savings plan
  • • Paid vacations, holidays and sick days
  • • Tuition reimbursement
  • • Online training resources

Career Development: Learn, Grow, and Lead

Our expanding Learning and Development program lets you take advantage of online and in-person programs to build new skills and advance your career.

Through in-house training and classes, our employees learn about the business of changing education, grow within our framework as we enrich the relationships between educators and students, and eventually lead the charge.

Popular courses include:

  • • Credo: Sharing Our Values and Principles with Each Other
  • • Coaching Learning Paths
  • • Purposeful Conversations & Feedback Workshop
  • • Building Your Financial Acumen
  • • Change Management Learning Paths
  • • New Manager Experience

We also offer tuition reimbursement, so you can build formal continuing education into your professional development plan.

An Ongoing Commitment to Accessibility

Cengage Learning is committed to increasing accessibility via a full spectrum of usable tools, features and choices that are accessible for users of all abilities.
